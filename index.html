About Me

I am an assistant professor in the Computer Science department at Kunsan National University, South Korea. My research focuses on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and AR/VR, specifically developing frameworks and interactive techniques to make spatial computing more accessible for daily activities.

Previously, I earned my Ph.D. at the University of Minnesota under Professor Daniel F. Keefe and completed a postdoc at the Texas Advanced Computing Center (TACC) under Paul Navrátil.
